Support the Mission

Support PNEC's Mission

PNEC is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit that operates entirely on donations and volunteer effort. Every dollar goes directly toward training, equipment, and keeping Poway's neighborhoods prepared.

Your Donation at Work

Here's how PNEC puts your support to use across Poway's 60+ neighborhoods.

📚

Training Materials

CERT manuals, first-aid reference cards, and neighborhood preparedness guides distributed to every coordinator and workshop participant.

🎒

CERT Equipment

Hard hats, safety vests, gloves, first-aid kits, and search lights for CERT response teams deployed during Poway emergencies.

🏘️

Community Outreach

Neighborhood fliers, community fair booths, multilingual preparedness materials, and door-to-door coordinator campaigns reaching thousands of households annually.

📻

Radio Infrastructure

PACT amateur radios, repeater maintenance, and equipment loans to licensed operators who form Poway's emergency communications backbone.
