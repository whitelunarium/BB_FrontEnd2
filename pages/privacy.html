Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who We Are

Poway Neighborhood Emergency Corps (PNEC) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization serving Poway, CA. This website is operated by PNEC volunteers to support community emergency preparedness.

Information We Collect

When you create a PNEC account, we collect your email address, display name, and optionally your neighborhood. We do not sell, rent, or share this information with third parties for commercial purposes.

If you submit a question through our FAQ system, we collect your name and email to allow staff to respond.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account and display your profile
  • To connect you with your neighborhood emergency coordinator (if you opt in)
  • To respond to questions you submit to PNEC staff
  • To send preparedness newsletters if you subscribe via Constant Contact (governed by their privacy policy)

Cookies & Sessions

We use a session cookie to keep you logged in. No tracking or advertising cookies are used on this site.

Third-Party Services

  • Open-Meteo — provides weather data for our risk widget. No user data is sent to Open-Meteo.
  • Leaflet / OpenStreetMap — provides map tiles for the neighborhood map. Map tiles are loaded from OpenStreetMap servers.
  • Constant Contact — manages our newsletter mailing list. See their privacy policy.

Data Security

Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and are never stored in plaintext. All data is stored on secured servers.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email info@powaynec.com.
