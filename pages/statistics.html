Poway Risk & Preparedness Statistics

Understanding local risk helps our community prepare. Here's what the data shows.

🔥 Wildfire Risk in Poway

High CAL FIRE Hazard Severity Zone designation for most of Poway
2007 Witch Creek Fire destroyed 1,100+ homes in Poway and nearby areas
60+ Neighborhoods in Poway that need a trained Emergency Coordinator
72 hrs FEMA recommendation for household self-sufficiency after a major disaster

Source: CAL FIRE, FEMA, San Diego County OES. Data updated 2024.

🌍 Earthquake Context

Rose Canyon Active fault system running through San Diego County, capable of M6.9+
~200 Minor earthquakes felt in San Diego County each year
M5.1 Largest recent earthquake near Poway (Anza area, 2020)

Source: USGS, Southern California Seismic Network. Data updated 2024.
