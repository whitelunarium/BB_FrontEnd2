About PNEC

Your Neighbors Are Your Best First Responders

In the critical minutes and hours after a disaster, professional help may not reach every neighborhood. PNEC trains and organizes Poway residents so neighbors can help each other — and help themselves.

We coordinate Neighborhood Emergency Coordinators across all of Poway's residential areas, offering CERT training, ham radio communication through our PACT program, and year-round preparedness education.
